CRYSTAL COLLECTOR



Instructions:

    You will be given a random number at the start of the game
    By clicking on one of the four crystals below, you will add a specific amount of points to your score.
    To win the game, you must match your score to that of the random number displayed. If your score exceeds the random number, you lose.
    Note: the value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
    Each time you start a new game, the random number and the values of the crystals will change.
